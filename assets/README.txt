ASSETS FOLDER
=============

Place your favicon files here:

  favicon.png     — 32x32 or 64x64 PNG favicon (referenced by all pages)
  favicon.svg     — SVG version (included as placeholder — replace with your design)
  favicon.ico     — ICO format for legacy browser support (optional)

How to add your custom favicon:
1. Design or obtain your favicon image (the Tumakazi T logo works well)
2. Convert to PNG at 32x32 and 64x64 sizes
3. Save as favicon.png in this folder
4. Upload to your hosting alongside all other files

Free favicon generators:
  - favicon.io
  - realfavicongenerator.net
  - convertio.co (to convert SVG to PNG/ICO)

The pages already reference: <link rel="icon" type="image/png" href="assets/favicon.png">
Add additional formats if needed:
  <link rel="icon" type="image/svg+xml" href="assets/favicon.svg">
  <link rel="shortcut icon" href="assets/favicon.ico">
